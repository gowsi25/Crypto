Visitors

This ticket admits one and is the general admission ticket that is best suited for solo marketers. It gives you access to all key parts of the conference like the marketplace, speeches, panels, and networking sessions on days one and two of the conference.

Delegate

This ticket admits one and is the general admission ticket that is especially for affiliate managers or representatives of an affiliate network, advertiser, traffic source, or agency. This ticket type gives you similar access as an Affiliate ticket but you'll be identified as a company, your company name, logo & description will be added on the Featured Companies page, and your logo will be printed directly on your badge.
